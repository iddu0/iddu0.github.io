Making PS2 Cover Art for CD Cases

Story time: I went to Value village to look around yesterday. I went to the books section (everything is always scattered around) and saw Gran Turismo 3 A-Spec for the PS2! Of course, I had to pick it up. I picked it up, but it wouldn't play, as the disc was a bit scratched. No biggie, I'll download it now that I legally own it.

But, I wanted a place to store the disc. I didn't have one of those DVD cases on hand (tall ones) so, I grabbed the next best thing, a CD case. The art wouldn't fit though! So, I Downloaded the art from a website, and I shrunk everything down so it would fit in the case. And, here's the final product!

Feel free to use this cover art for free. I might make a back, and more cover arts!
