Downloading a Folder

Since GitHub doesn't allow you to download Folders, you have to do something else. Here's how to download a Folder.
Step 1. Click the Files button to go to the files.
Step 2. Go to the folder and copy the link/URL
Step 3. Click here, and paste the Folders link into the box and hit enter
You are now done! The folder should download as a ZIP folder
